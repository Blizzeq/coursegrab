How to get CAUTH
  1. Open coursera.org and log in to your account
  2. Open DevTools: F12 or Cmd+Opt+I
  3. Go to Application tab (Chrome) or Storage tab (Firefox)
  4. In the sidebar: Cookies > https://www.coursera.org
  5. Find the cookie named CAUTH
  6. Double-click its Value, copy, and paste here

The cookie expires periodically — grab a fresh one if downloads fail with auth errors.
